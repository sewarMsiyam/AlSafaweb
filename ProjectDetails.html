Project Goals

  1. Providing 20,000 copies of the Noble Qur’an containing a translation of its meanings into the Filipino language.
  2. Filling the severe shortage in the number of copies of the Qur’an that contain translations of its meanings in mosques, schools and houses of the Qur’an in the Philippines.
  3. Providing high quality printed copies of the Qur'an.
  4. Enabling the target group to understand the Noble Qur’an by publishing the Qur’an that contains translation or an easy interpretation of the meanings of the words of the Noble Qur’an.
  5. Introducing the Qur’an, which contains the translation of meanings in the Filipino language, to the largest possible number of mosques, schools, and homes in remote areas of the Philippines.
