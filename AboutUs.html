About Our Company

Al-Safa Academy for Quranic Services: It is a Malaysian institution of origin, concerned with teaching the Holy Quran and its sciences to young people, teachers, imams and those interested. The Academy was established in the second quarter of 2018. It is an academy that believes in the necessity of integrating creativity into the educational process; By taking care of the three pillars of education: the teacher - the student - the curriculum.
